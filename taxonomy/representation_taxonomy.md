# Representation Taxonomy

- **Observation space:** future sensor values or engineered measurements.
- **Continuous latent space:** dense learned state vectors.
- **Discrete latent/token space:** quantized states or tokens.
- **Hybrid physical-latent space:** learned states constrained or supplemented by physical variables.
- **Graph-structured state:** components and relations represented as a graph.
- **Multimodal state:** sensor, image, text, maintenance, or operating-context signals combined.

Record state semantics, observability, temporal resolution, and whether the representation supports intervention or uncertainty analysis.

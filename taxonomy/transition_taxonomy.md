# Transition Taxonomy

Transition mechanisms may be deterministic, probabilistic, stochastic latent-variable, state-space, autoregressive, non-autoregressive, action-conditioned, physics-informed, retrieval-augmented, or continuous-time. Multiple labels are allowed when their roles are explained.

For each method, record whether transitions are explicit or implicit, learned or specified, one-step or multi-step, open-loop or corrected by observations, and whether action or operating-condition inputs affect evolution.

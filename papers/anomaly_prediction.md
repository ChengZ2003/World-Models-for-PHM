# Anomaly Prediction and Early Warning

This page covers methods that estimate anomaly risk in a future horizon separated from the observation window by an explicit lead-time gap.

## Inclusion Considerations

The target horizon and lead-time gap should be explicit, and no future target information may enter feature construction. Current-window detection alone is not early warning.

# Health Assessment

This page covers dynamic health-state and health-index estimation when the state or transition mechanism is relevant to downstream prognosis.

## Inclusion Considerations

A health index alone is not sufficient for world-model classification; curators should examine whether it participates in a learned transition or rollout.

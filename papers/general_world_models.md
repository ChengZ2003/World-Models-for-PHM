# General World Models

This page defines the background scope needed to discuss system-state representation, dynamics learning, rollout, planning, and uncertainty. Inclusion in this background category does not imply direct PHM evaluation.

## Inclusion Considerations

Background work must clarify a mechanism used by the survey. Generic generative or forecasting work without such relevance should not be added merely for coverage.
